RESEARCH

ACTIVE & PLANNED PROJECTS

🔬 Lateral Flow Assay (LFA) Reader for Early Cancer Detection
Active Research

Developing a smartphone-integrated LFA reader capable of detecting cancer biomarkers from blood samples at an early stage. This project combines microfluidics, paper-based biosensors, and AI-powered image analysis to create a low-cost, portable diagnostic tool accessible even in rural healthcare settings. The goal is to achieve sensitivity matching clinical-grade ELISA assays at a fraction of the cost.

💉 Needle-less Vaccine Delivery Technology
Active Research

Exploring transdermal and microneedle patch-based delivery systems for vaccines that eliminate the need for syringes. This research investigates biocompatible polymer matrices, dissolving microneedle arrays, and thermoresponsive drug release mechanisms. The objective is to improve vaccine compliance, reduce needle-stick injuries, and enable cold-chain-free immunization in remote areas.

🤖 KLU Student AI Agent (RAG-powered)
Ongoing Development

Built a LangChain + LangGraph based AI agent backed by ChromaDB and FastAPI that enables KL University students to query academic records, extracurricular data, and competitive programming statistics via natural language. Implements k=3 retrieval-augmented generation with a locally-run Ollama (Qwen2) LLM.
